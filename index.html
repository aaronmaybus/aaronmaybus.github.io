Me, Aaron

Starting out in the performing arts, Aaron worked as a part of original bands as a bass guitarist and backing vocalist, also alongside photographers, video producers and artists. After High School he attained a Diploma & Bachelor of Contemporary Music Performance at the Australian College of the Arts (Collarts) in South Melbourne. After working as apart of the Melbourne creative industry he chased his aspirations to Brisbane, Australia.

Aaron has photographed and produced videos and performed for a wide range of events & artists ranging from Original bands, Club Scenes to Gala Events throughout Melbourne and Brisbane, Australia. Aaron has worked with 120 Publishing, Ministry of Sound, Poncho TV, Sink or Swim Productions (Medibank, Multiple recording artists, Melodic Music, Queensland Brian Institute, QLD Charities (contact for more details) & many entertainment companies in Queensland.

Currently, Aaron is a freelancer in Brisbane, Queensland producing content for different projects across Photography, Video & Performance for a wide range of companies, charities and music associations.

With his extensive experience, Aaron knows how to read a room and make sure your event is one to remember.
